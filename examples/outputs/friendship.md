## Story

OUTLINE:
Setup: Alice and her best friend Bob the cat live in a cozy cottage by the meadow.
Small problem: Bob accidentally spills milk on Alice's favorite book.
Kind resolution: Alice realizes Bob didn't mean to and they work together to clean up the mess.
Wind-down: Alice snuggles up with Bob, feeling grateful for their friendship.

STORY:
In a small cottage by the meadow, Alice and her best friend Bob the cat spent their days exploring the woods and chasing butterflies. They shared everything, from afternoon naps in the sun to secret whispers under the twinkling stars.

One sunny morning, as Alice sat by the window reading her favorite book, Bob leaped onto the table, knocking over a glass of milk. The white liquid spilled all over the pages, making them soggy and stained. Alice gasped and looked at Bob, who blinked innocently.

"Oh, Bob!" Alice exclaimed, trying to hide her disappointment. "You spilled milk on my book."

Bob meowed softly, rubbing his head against Alice's leg, trying to apologize. Alice sighed, realizing Bob didn't mean to cause a mess. She fetched a cloth and started wiping the milky drops, Bob watching her intently.

As they cleaned the pages together, Alice smiled at Bob. "It's okay, Bob. I know you didn't do it on purpose. Accidents happen sometimes, even to the best of friends."

Bob purred in contentment, happy that Alice wasn't upset with him. They finished cleaning the book, the pages slightly crinkled but the words still legible. Alice hugged Bob tightly, grateful for his furry companionship.

As the sun dipped below the horizon, casting a warm glow over the cottage, Alice and Bob curled up on the soft rug by the fireplace. Bob's gentle purring filled the room, lulling Alice into a peaceful slumber. In the comfort of their friendship, Alice drifted off to sleep, feeling safe and loved.
