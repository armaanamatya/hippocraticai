## Story

OUTLINE:
- Setup: In the kingdom of Brightwood, a brave little knight named Arthur sets off on a quest to find a magical flower.
- Small problem: Along the way, Arthur gets lost in the enchanted forest.
- Kind resolution: Arthur meets a friendly squirrel named Hazel, who teaches him that asking for help is a kind of courage.
- Wind-down: With Hazel's guidance, Arthur finds the magical flower and returns safely to the kingdom.

STORY:
Once upon a time, in the kingdom of Brightwood, there lived a brave little knight named Arthur. One sunny morning, Arthur decided to embark on a quest to find a rare and magical flower that was said to bring light to the darkest of places. With his shiny armor gleaming in the sunlight, Arthur set off into the enchanted forest, determined to find the flower.

As Arthur journeyed deeper into the forest, the trees grew taller and the shadows longer. Soon, he found himself surrounded by unfamiliar paths, and before he knew it, he had lost his way. Feeling a bit worried, Arthur sat down on a mossy log and wondered how he would ever find the magical flower now.

Just then, a chittering sound caught Arthur's attention. He looked up to see a friendly squirrel named Hazel peering down at him from a branch above. "Hello there, young knight! Are you lost?" asked Hazel with a kind smile.

Arthur nodded sheepishly, feeling a bit embarrassed. "I'm trying to find the magical flower, but I seem to have lost my way," he admitted.

Hazel hopped down from the branch and offered to help Arthur navigate through the forest. As they walked together, Hazel shared stories of the forest and its wonders, making Arthur forget all about his worries. Along the way, Arthur learned that asking for help was not a sign of weakness, but a kind of courage that brought people together.

With Hazel's guidance, Arthur finally reached the spot where the magical flower bloomed in a burst of light and color. Grateful for Hazel's help, Arthur picked the flower and thanked his new friend for showing him the way.

As the sun began to set, Arthur bid farewell to Hazel and made his way back to the kingdom of Brightwood, his heart full of gratitude and newfound courage. And as he drifted off to sleep that night, Arthur knew that asking for help was indeed a brave and noble thing to do.
